## Purpose

robots.txt (crawl mechanics) and llms.txt (content summary for LLMs).

## Site Description
STS Futures is a web-based dashboard providing systematic NQ
(Nasdaq-100 E-mini) futures trading signals with:
- 15+ years of backtested performance data (2011-2026)
- Approximately +1,085% total return, ~46% win rate, Sharpe ~1.07,
  ~51% maximum drawdown over the backtest window
- Real-time trade signal alerts on the dashboard plus optional email
- Professional analytics (equity curves, drawdown overlay, Sharpe,
  Sortino, calendar P&L heatmap)
- Full trade-by-trade history (~7,900 historical trades)
- $50/month subscription, cancel anytime, no free trial

## Primary Topics (relevant to STS)
- NQ (Nasdaq-100) futures trading
- Systematic / algorithmic intraday trading signals
- Intraday futures trading workflow
- Trading performance analytics + risk-adjusted metrics
- Position sizing + drawdown survival
- Comparison: signal services vs prop firms vs broker-bundled algos

## Topics STS Does NOT Cover
- Stocks, options, ETFs, crypto (futures only)
- Markets other than NQ (no ES, CL, GC, BTC)
- Tax advice or filing
- Personalized financial planning
- Discord / Telegram chat groups
- Auto-execution to broker
- Trading education courses (FAQ + guides only)
