# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

## Refund Policy for STS Futures

### Free Trial

- **Duration**: 10 free trading alerts
- **Refund**: Not applicable (free service)
- **Cancellation**: You can cancel your account at any time during the trial period

### Paid Subscriptions

#### Monthly Subscription

- **Billing Cycle**: Monthly, recurring on the same date each month
- **Refund Window**: 7 days from the date of purchase
- **Refund Eligibility**: Full refund if requested within 7 days of initial purchase
- **After 7 Days**: No refunds for partial months; you must cancel before the next billing cycle

#### Annual Subscription

- **Billing Cycle**: Once per year
- **Refund Window**: 30 days from the date of purchase
- **Refund Eligibility**: Full refund if requested within 30 days of initial purchase
- **After 30 Days**: No refunds; however, you can cancel to prevent future renewals

### How to Request a Refund

1. Log into your account at https://intradaydash-jfmy8c2b.manus.space
2. Navigate to Settings → Billing
3. Click "Request Refund"
4. Provide a reason for your refund request
5. Submit the form

Alternatively, email: refunds@stsfutures.com with:

- Your account email address
- Order date and transaction ID
- Reason for refund request

### Refund Processing

- **Processing Time**: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- **Refund Method**: Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- **Partial Refunds**: Not available for partial months; refunds are full or none

### Non-Refundable Items

The following are NOT eligible for refund:

- Trading losses or losses resulting from using our signals
- Fees charged by your broker for trade execution
- Commissions or slippage
- Margin interest or financing charges
- Taxes or other government fees
- Subscriptions purchased more than 7 days ago (monthly) or 30 days ago (annual)

### Cancellation vs. Refund

**Cancellation**: Stops future billing without refund

- Available at any time
- Takes effect at the end of current billing cycle
- No refund issued

**Refund**: Returns payment for recent purchase

- Only available within refund window (7 or 30 days)
- Processed immediately
- Cancels subscription

### Special Circumstances

#### Service Outages

If our service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours due to our fault, you may request a prorated refund for the affected period.

#### Billing Errors

If you were charged incorrectly, we will:

1. Issue a refund for the erroneous charge
2. Correct your billing information
3. Prevent future billing errors

Contact: billing@stsfutures.com

#### Account Termination

If we terminate your account due to:

- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity
- Abuse of the service

No refund will be issued.

### Chargeback Policy

If you initiate a chargeback through your credit card company:

1. Your account will be suspended immediately
2. You forfeit any remaining subscription benefits
3. You may be permanently banned from using our service
4. We reserve the right to pursue legal action

We encourage you to contact us first to resolve any billing disputes.

**Disputes**: support@stsfutures.com

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to this page. Your continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
